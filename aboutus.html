About Us

Welcome to ERA Hospitals!

ERA Hospital is a leading multi-specialty, tertiary care Hospital that lays its foundation on a ‘Patient first’ ideology. The hospital caters to all branches of medicine right from birthing and new born care up to cancer and multi-organ transplants. It follows a Greenfield over Brownfield strategy for delivering leading-edge healthcare to cater to the changing needs of the growing populace.

Situated in the vicinity of the arterial Eastern Express Highway, ERA Hospital is the epitome of medical innovations and quality healthcare that offers easy connectivity and accessibility to patients from all the nodes across Thane and Pune.
